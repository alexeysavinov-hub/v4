03 / STORY

Our story

From a handful of veterans to a 100-strong studio with 10 million players.

2020

We came together as a small team of professionals who had shipped Dragons World, Breaking Bad, Narcos, Army of Heroes and Hustle Castle.

Our first project was Broyalty — a mid-core strategy of battles and castle development. Despite promising metrics and high retention, it fell short of expectations and moved to maintenance mode.

2022

In August 2022 we founded TOP APP GAMES and began developing Ludus: Merge Arena — merge mechanics meet battler, built around repeatable gameplay and a deep collection of card mechanics.

2023

The first version launched in May 2023 to a high retention rate. We spent the following months relentlessly improving the game.

2024

Worldwide launch in May 2024 — and the 1 million player milestone soon after. A major step forward for the studio.

2025

LUDUS grew exponentially to 7 million players. We expanded the team and started work on two new projects.

2026

LUDUS passed 10 million downloads, Siege Arena entered the arena — and our third title, the one we believe will be our masterpiece, is in production.
